Asymmetric Encryption
C++

As part of a larger project, I designed and implemented an Encryption Algorithm. It uses two public keys, a private key and a checksum to encode an inputted string. It would take 9^(2n-1) attempts to bruteforce the encyption where 'n' is the length of the string. This means that the average sentence would take 2.95e+94 attempts to bruteforce. A later update added the ability to encode a greater amount of characters.
